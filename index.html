⚡ Hook Bomb (viral text-on-screen reel)
The #1 faceless format. One line per beat — line 1 is the HOOK (it's also the first frame, for sound-off scrollers). Use *gold* _red_ +green+ marks. Keep beats under 8 words. Under 15s total.
⏱ Challenge Reel (guess before the timer)
Retention machine: question → 3-2-1 countdown → answer snap. Viewers must stay to check answers, then comment their score. First line = title, then one Question | Answer per line (3-5 questions).
💬 Chat Story (text-message reel — top viral faceless format)
A conversation that types out bubble by bubble. Line 1 = contact name. Then A: (them, grey) and B: (you, gold) lines. First message must shock — it's the first frame.
📊 Stat Slam (numbers that count up and hit)
Data drama for your facts niche: each stat counts up with an odometer slam. Line 1 = hook title. Then Label | Number | suffix | context line.
⚔️ This or That (vote-bait — comments engineered in)
Rapid A-vs-B rounds. Viewers HAVE to pick a side in the comments. Line 1 = title. Then Option A | Option B per line.
🎞 Scene Reel (script + images + charts, synced)
The director format: mix TEXT: beats, IMG: photos (Ken Burns motion), BAR: animated charts, and NUM: counting stats — one line per scene, visuals land exactly on script.
▶️ YouTube Pack (thumbnail + search metadata)
Your reel MP4s are already Shorts-ready. This adds the YouTube layer: a 1280×720 thumbnail for long-form uploads, and SEO metadata — YouTube is a search engine, packaging decides clicks.
